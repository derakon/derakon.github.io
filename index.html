Features

  • Fast-paced arcade naval action pitting your lone ship against dozens of enemy ships, submarines, and airplanes.
  • Extensive singleplayer campaign, broken up into small pick-up-and-play missions.
  • Powerful ship designer that allows you to freely place parts on your ship's deck, along with customizable flags, paint jobs, and decals. Build your own destroyer, cruiser, or battleship!
  • Dozens of playable ships inspired by historical designs.
  • Hundreds of ship parts with distinct artwork and capabilities. Use naval artillery, rockets, torpedoes, anti-aircraft guns, missile launchers, and more advanced weaponry like railguns and lasers to take down the enemy!
  • Story-driven missions with a small cast of heroes and villains. Circumnavigate the globe in your fight against tyrrany!
